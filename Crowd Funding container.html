Success Fee

Our success is aligned to yours, so we only charge an exit fee if you make a profit on your investment; there are no annual fees for the ongoing support we provide once you've made an investment. If you make a return on your investment, you'll keep 95% of any profit, and we'll take a 5% fee for managing your investement over the years.

The success fee, which, sometimes also known as 'carry', covers everything we do to find India's hottest high-growth businesses for you to invest in, and all the work we undertake holding and managing your shareholding on your behalf over the years.

While it's free to join our investor community, there is a 1.5% charge applied to the amount you invest, which is subject to a minimum of 0.50 and is capped at a maximum of 250.00

Crowdcube's success fee only applies to profits made on investments in businesses that opened to investment on or after the 1st April 2022.
